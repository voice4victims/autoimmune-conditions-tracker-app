# Product Overview

This is a PANDAS (Pediatric Autoimmune Neuropsychiatric Disorders Associated with Streptococcal Infections) symptom tracking application. It's a comprehensive medical tracking tool designed for parents and caregivers to monitor children's symptoms, treatments, and health data.

## Key Features

- **Child Profile Management**: Multi-child support with individual profiles
- **Symptom Tracking**: Daily symptom logging with custom symptom support
- **Treatment Monitoring**: Track medications, supplements, and treatment effectiveness
- **Data Visualization**: Charts, heatmaps, and analytics for symptom patterns
- **Medical Records**: File management, provider tracking, appointment booking
- **Family Collaboration**: Multi-user access with role-based permissions
- **Health Monitoring**: Vital signs, food diary, activity tracking
- **Security**: Encrypted data storage with Firebase backend

## Target Users

Parents and caregivers of children with PANDAS/PANS conditions who need to track complex medical data and share information with healthcare providers.

## Medical Context

The app includes medical disclaimers and emphasizes that it's a tracking tool, not a diagnostic or treatment platform. All medical decisions should be made in consultation with healthcare professionals.
