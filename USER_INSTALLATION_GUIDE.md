## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Is this a real app?
Yes! This is a Progressive Web App (PWA) - a modern type of app that works just like apps from the App Store or Google Play, but installs directly from the web.

### Do I need to download anything?
No downloads needed! Just visit the website and add it to your home screen. It's that simple.

### Will it work without internet?
Yes! Once installed, the app works offline. Your data is saved on your device and syncs when you're back online.

### Is my data secure?
Absolutely! Your data is encrypted and stored securely using Firebase, the same technology used by major apps like Spotify and The New York Times.

### How do I update the app?
Updates happen automatically! Just open the app and it will update itself in the background.

### Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes! Log in with the same account on any device and your data will sync automatically.

### How much does it cost?
The app is completely free to use!

### What if I have problems?
Contact support or check the FAQ section in the app for help.

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" option not showing (iPhone)
- Make sure you're using **Safari** (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Update to the latest iOS version
- Try clearing Safari cache

### Installation not working (Android)
- Make sure you're using **Chrome** browser
- Update Chrome to the latest version
- Check that you have enough storage space

### App not loading
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try reinstalling the app

### Forgot password
- Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen
- Check your email for reset instructions
